# Privacy Notice

## Data Collection and Storage

Wheel of Names does not collect, sell, or share personal data with third parties.

### Local Processing
All names you enter and any center image you upload are processed locally in your browser session for add-on functionality only.

### Local Storage
The add-on may store your wheel state locally on your device using browser local storage so your current setup can be reopened in future sessions. This is disabled if you clear browser data.

### No Tracking
The add-on does not use:
- Analytics trackers
- Advertising pixels
- User profiling services
- Remote telemetry or logging

### Third-Party Platforms
For Adobe Express platform-level data practices and privacy, please refer to [Adobe's Privacy Policy](https://www.adobe.com/privacy.html).

### Questions
If you have privacy concerns about this add-on, please open an issue on GitHub or contact the developer.
